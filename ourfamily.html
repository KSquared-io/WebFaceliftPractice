Our Family

The Mandara Men (from left) Augusto ‘Augie’ Mandara, Michele Mandara, Antonino ‘Tony’ Mandara, Owners-Chefs at Villa Monte Pizzeria & Restaurant in Staten Island, NY.

Montepertuso of Positano, Italy. Birthplace and hometown of Augusto and Antonino until 1971, when the family moved to the United States.

Leaving behind their close-knit community in Montepertuso of Positano, Italy, Michele Mandara, along with his family, ventured to the United States in hopes of a better life. Together with his sons, Augusto ‘Augie’ and Antonino ‘Tony,’ they laid the foundation for Villa Monte Pizzeria and Restaurant, the true meaning of their home away from home. After successfully establishing their first location on Richmond Avenue, Villa Monte has expanded itself to most of Staten Island with its other three locations. Whether behind the counter, making pizza, greeting customers, serving a glass of wine, or answering the phones for one of the hundreds of deliveries a day, you will always find Chef-Owner Augie at their ‘home base’ Richmond Avenue location with a welcoming smile on his face. As the Italian saying goes, Mangia Bene, Beve Bene, Si Sente Bene, Villa Monte prides itself on you entering as guests, staying as friends, and leaving as family – all as you Eat Well, Drink Well, and Feel Well!
